# Summary

This designs allows you to place a layer fan integrated with the E3D V6 own fan.   
This support consists of two parts:  
  - **Fan layer support**  
  - **E3D fan support** (**open**: "easy to place whit the hotend place on the extruder"; **close**: "to mount before placing the hot end")  
Layer fan size: 40 x 40 [mm]   
E3D fan size: 30 x 30 [mm]   
The design is a mixture of two designs:   
- http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:344950  
- http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:465495
